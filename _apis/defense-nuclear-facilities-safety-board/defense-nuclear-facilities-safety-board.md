  The Defense Nuclear Facilities Safety Board is an independent organization
  within the executive branch of the United States Government, chartered with
  the responsibility of providing recommendations and advice to the President
  and the Secretary of Energy regarding public health and safety issues at
  Department of Energy defense nuclear facilities.
