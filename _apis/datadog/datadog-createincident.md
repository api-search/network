description: >-
  Creates a new incident record in Datadog Incident Management. An incident captures an outage or issue, including its title, severity, status, customer impact description, and associated fields. Creating an incident triggers notifications to configured responders and creates a dedicated Slack channel if configured. The incident is assigned a unique ID and timestamped at creation time. Custom fields configured in your organization settings can be set on incident creation.
