description: >-
  Species of Greatest Conservation Need are lists of species designated in the
  56 State Wildlife Action Plans which identify the species most in need of
  conservation action in that state or U.S. territory. USGS developed a process
  that allows these lists to be compiled into a national list, making their
  representation across taxa groups available via the Species Conservation
  Analysis Tool.
