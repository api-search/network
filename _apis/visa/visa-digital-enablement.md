  The Visa Digital Enablement (VDE) Lite App is a standalone, white-label
  solution that enables cardholders to push card credentials into Apple Pay (for
  iOS users) or Google Wallet (for Android users) without the need to download a
  client's mobile app. This efficient user experience facilitates quick and
  seamless execution of targeted tasks like provisioning to a digital wallet,
  thereby enabling immediate use of the card.
