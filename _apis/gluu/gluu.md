description: >-
  Gluu provides an open-source, cloud-native identity and access management
  platform that lets organizations secure web and mobile apps, APIs, and data
  with standards-based single sign-on, strong authentication, and fine-grained
  authorization. It implements protocols such as OpenID Connect, OAuth 2.0,
  SAML, FIDO2/WebAuthn, and UMA, integrates with directories like LDAP/Active
  Directory, and supports MFA, identity federation, and social login to deliver
  a unified sign-on experience. Deployed on Kubernetes or virtual machines, Gluu
  scales for both workforce and customer IAM use cases, offers policy-driven
  access control and consent, and includes tooling for administration and user
  self-service. The company also provides enterprise support and services around
  its distribution (e.g., Gluu Flex), which is built on the open-source Janssen
  Project.
