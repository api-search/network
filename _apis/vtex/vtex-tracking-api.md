  shipments in real-time. By entering the tracking number provided by the
  sender, customers can access detailed information about the status and
  location of their delivery. Vtex Tracking provides updates on estimated
  delivery times, any delays or issues with the shipment, as well as
  notifications when the package has been successfully delivered. This service
  helps users stay informed and informed about the progress of their orders,
  giving them peace of mind and ensuring a smooth delivery experience.
