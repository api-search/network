  Vtex's platform, businesses can customize their gift card programs to suit

  codes, and monitoring the usage and balance of each card. Overall, the Vtex
  Gift Card Provider Protocol streamlines the gift card process, making it a
  seamless and efficient tool for businesses to increase customer engagement and
  boost sales.
