  erase, or restrict the processing of their personal data. This tool helps
  companies manage and respond to data subject requests in a timely and
  efficient manner, ensuring compliance with regulations and maintaining trust
  with customers. By streamlining the process of handling data subject rights,
  Vtex Data Subject Rights helps organizations demonstrate their commitment to
  data privacy and protection.
