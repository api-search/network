description: >-
  Common Sense Media is a nonprofit organization that provides parents and
  educators with unbiased information and tools to navigate the digital world
  and media landscape. They offer reviews, ratings, and recommendations for
  movies, TV shows, books, video games, apps, and websites, helping families
  make informed decisions about what content is appropriate for their children.
  Common Sense Media also provides resources and advice on topics such as online
  safety, cyberbullying, and digital literacy, empowering families to have
  healthy and productive conversations about media consumption. Overall, their
  mission is to promote responsible and safe media use for children and families
  in today's digital age.
