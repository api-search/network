description: >-
  Issues a full or partial refund on a settled transaction. Partial refunds may be issued multiple times until the cumulative refunded amount equals the settled amount. A new transaction of type "credit" is created representing the refund. The original transaction must be in a settled or settling status to be eligible for refund.
