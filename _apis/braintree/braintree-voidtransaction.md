description: >-
  Voids an authorized or submitted-for-settlement transaction, preventing it from being settled. Voiding cancels the payment before funds are transferred. A transaction can only be voided if it is in the authorized, submitted_for_settlement, or settlement_pending status. Once voided, a transaction cannot be captured or refunded.
