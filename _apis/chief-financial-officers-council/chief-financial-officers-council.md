  The Council was established by the Chief Financial Officers (CFO) Act of 1990
  (Public Law 101-576). It is composed of the CFOs and Deputy CFOs of the
  largest Federal agencies, senior officials of OMB, and Treasury who work
  collaboratively to improve financial management in the U.S. Government.
