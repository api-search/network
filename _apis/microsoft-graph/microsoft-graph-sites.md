description: >-
  Microsoft Graph Sites is the Graph API surface for SharePoint Online, letting
  apps discover and work with sites and their content through a single, secure
  endpoint. It enables you to locate the root site or a site by URL, search for
  sites, and navigate a sites structure to access document libraries (drives),
  lists, list items, files, and modern site pages. You can read and update
  metadata, columns, and content types; follow or unfollow sites; work with the
  term store (taxonomy); and relate a site to connected resources like users,
  groups, and Teams. Because its part of Microsoft Graph, you get consistent
  authentication, granular permissions, and cross-service capabilities, plus
  change tracking and webhooks on supported resources to build reliable,
  automated content and intranet solutions.
