description: >-
  The FinOps Foundation is a vendor-neutral community under the Linux Foundation
  that advances the practice of cloud financial management (FinOps). It stewards
  the FinOps Framework, common language, and best practices that help
  engineering, finance, and business teams collaborate to manage cloud cost and
  value. The foundation convenes a global community through working groups,
  events, and an active Slack; publishes research such as the annual State of
  FinOps; and maintains standards and open specifications like FOCUS to
  normalize cost and usage data. It also offers training and certifications for
  practitioners and platforms, a tools landscape, and practical guidance to help
  organizations progress from visibility to optimization to ongoing operations
  at scale.
