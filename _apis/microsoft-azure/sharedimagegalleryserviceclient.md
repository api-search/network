  Microsoft Azure Shared Image Gallery Service Client is a tool that allows
  users to efficiently create, manage, and share custom virtual machine images
  across their organization. With this service, users can easily capture and
  deploy images of their virtual machines, ensuring consistency and reliability
  across all instances. The Shared Image Gallery Service Client also provides
  advanced features such as versioning, replication, and access controls,
  allowing users to securely store and distribute images to specific teams or
  departments. By centralizing and streamlining image management, this service
  helps organizations save time and resources in their cloud computing
  operations.
