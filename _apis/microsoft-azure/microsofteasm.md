  Microsoft Azure External Attack Surface Management helps organizations
  identify and mitigate potential security risks by continuously monitoring and
  assessing their external attack surface. This includes identifying all
  external-facing assets such as web applications, APIs, and network devices,
  and assessing their security posture to detect vulnerabilities and
  misconfigurations. By providing real-time insights and alerts on potential
  threats, Azure External Attack Surface Management enables organizations to
  proactively defend against cyber attacks and protect their critical assets.
  Additionally, it helps organizations comply with industry regulations and
  standards by ensuring the security of their external attack surface meets
  required security benchmarks. Overall, Microsoft Azure External Attack Surface
  Management is a crucial tool for enhancing cybersecurity posture and reducing
  the risk of external threats.
