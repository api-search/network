  resources deployed within Azure. The Policy Client enables users to create,
  assign, and manage policies across their entire Azure subscription, ensuring
  that resources are consistently deployed in accordance with organizational
  standards. By using Azure Policy Client, users can easily monitor and enforce
  compliance, helping to maintain a secure and well-managed Azure environment.
