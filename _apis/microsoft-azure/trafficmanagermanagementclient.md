  The Microsoft Azure Traffic Manager Management Client is a tool designed to
  help users effectively manage and monitor their Azure Traffic Manager
  services. With this client, users can easily create, update, and delete
  traffic manager profiles, endpoints, and monitor the health of their traffic
  manager resources. Additionally, the management client allows users to
  configure traffic routing policies, such as weighted, priority, and geographic
  routing, to optimize performance and ensure high availability of their
  applications and services. In essence, the Microsoft Azure Traffic Manager
  Management Client helps users efficiently control and optimize their network
  traffic for improved performance and reliability.
