  Microsoft Azure Maps Wayfinding Service is a cloud-based service that provides
  interactive maps and navigation capabilities for users to easily create and
  use custom wayfinding experiences in their applications. With this service,
  developers can integrate real-time directions, route optimization, and
  location-based services into their applications, allowing users to navigate
  complex indoor and outdoor environments with ease. Azure Maps Wayfinding
  Service leverages a rich set of geographic data and advanced algorithms to
  provide accurate and efficient routing options, helping users find the
  quickest and most convenient route to their destination. Additionally, the
  service offers customizable styling options and interactive features for a
  tailored wayfinding experience that meets the specific needs of users and
  organizations.
