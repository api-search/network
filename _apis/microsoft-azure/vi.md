  Microsoft Azure Virtual Instruments (Vi) is a cloud-based platform that
  provides teams with the ability to monitor, analyze, and optimize the
  performance of their applications and services. With Vi, users can track key
  performance metrics, identify potential bottlenecks or errors, and make
  data-driven decisions to improve overall efficiency and reliability. The
  platform offers real-time monitoring and diagnostics, as well as customizable
  performance alerts and reporting capabilities. By leveraging the insights
  provided by Vi, teams can proactively address issues, enhance user experience,
  and ultimately optimize the performance of their cloud-based environments on
  Microsoft Azure.
