  The Microsoft Azure Subscriptions Management Client is a tool that helps users
  effectively manage their Azure subscriptions. It allows users to easily view
  and update their subscription details, such as billing information, usage
  reports, and access control. With this client, users can easily track their
  resource usage, allocate costs, and optimize their subscription plans to fit
  their specific needs. Additionally, the Subscriptions Management Client
  provides a centralized dashboard for users to monitor and analyze their
  subscription data, making it easier for them to make informed decisions about
  their Azure resources. Overall, this client streamlines the process of
  managing Azure subscriptions, improving efficiency and control for users.
