  It provides a centralized platform where administrators can create, manage,

  compliance with internal standards, industry regulations, and best practices.
  This tool helps to streamline governance processes, mitigate security risks,
  and maintain consistency across the Azure environment. Ultimately, the Azure
  Policy States Client empowers organizations to optimize the performance,
  security, and efficiency of their cloud infrastructure.
