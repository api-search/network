  Microsoft Azure Management Lock Client is a tool that allows users to apply
  locks to their Azure resources to prevent accidental deletion or modification.
  This can help ensure the stability and security of critical resources within
  an organization's Azure environment. The Management Lock Client provides a
  simple and intuitive interface for users to create, manage, and remove locks
  on their resources, giving them greater control and visibility over their
  Azure infrastructure. By utilizing this tool, users can protect their
  resources from unauthorized changes and maintain the integrity of their cloud
  environment.
