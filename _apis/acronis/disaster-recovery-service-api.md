  businesses to easily integrate disaster recovery capabilities into their
  existing workflows, enabling faster recovery times and minimizing downtime in
  the event of a disaster. With this API, organizations can ensure the
  protection and availability of their data and applications, giving them peace
  of mind and confidence in their disaster recovery plans.
