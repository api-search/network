description: Mastercard_ Merchant Presented QR (MPQR) is a Mastercard QR program providing a consumer-initiated, mobile payment solution. MPQR enables consumers to make cashless payments for goods and services from their mobile phones by simply scanning a Mastercard QR Code or manually inputting an alias provided by any Mastercard QR accepting merchant. This push payment method has the convenience of cash and the security of a card payment, without needing a card payment terminal.
