  to sensitive data and resources. By utilizing the Okta Authenticators API,
  organizations can enhance the security of their platforms and protect against
  unauthorized access and fraudulent activities. This API provides a flexible
  and customizable solution for businesses looking to bolster their security
  measures and safeguard their digital assets.
