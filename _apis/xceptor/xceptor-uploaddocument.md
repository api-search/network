description: >-
  Uploads a document to the Xceptor platform for processing. The document is accepted in its original format and queued for classification and extraction. Supported formats include PDF, Microsoft Excel, CSV, XML, JSON, email (EML/MSG), and image files (PNG, JPG, TIFF). Documents can optionally be assigned to a specific extraction template.
