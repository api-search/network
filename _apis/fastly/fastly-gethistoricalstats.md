description: >-
  Retrieves historical statistics for all Fastly services associated with the account. Data with day resolution is bucketed based on UTC days and becomes available around 2 AM the following day. Hour resolution becomes available approximately 15 minutes after the end of each hour. Minute data usually becomes available two minutes after the end of the minute. Minutely data older than 35 days and hourly data older than 375 days are not available.
