description: >-
  The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Decision Reviews API is a tool that
  allows veterans and their advocates to access information regarding decisions
  made by the VA on claims for benefits and services. The API provides a
  platform for users to review and track the status of their claims, as well as
  access detailed information on the reasoning behind the VA's decisions. This
  allows veterans to better understand the decision-making process and provides
  transparency in how claims are reviewed and evaluated. Additionally, the API
  allows for easier communication between veterans, their advocates, and the VA,
  streamlining the claims process and ensuring that veterans receive the
  benefits and services they are entitled to in a timely manner.
