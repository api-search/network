  organizations to automate repetitive tasks and processes within their Azure
  environment. It provides a centralized platform for creating, scheduling, and
  managing automation workflows, allowing users to streamline their operations
  and improve efficiency. With Azure Automation Management, users can automate
  tasks such as provisioning and managing resources, running scripts, and
  orchestrating complex workflows. This helps to reduce human error, increase
  consistency, and free up valuable time for IT teams to focus on more strategic
  initiatives. Overall, Azure Automation Management simplifies the management of
  cloud infrastructure and improves overall operational effectiveness.
