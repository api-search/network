  sources and energy-efficient data centers, Azure Carbon provides an
  environmentally friendly solution for companies looking to transition to a

  offsetting capabilities, Azure Carbon enables organizations to track and
  reduce their carbon emissions while still meeting their business needs. By
  leveraging Azure Carbon, businesses can not only minimize their environmental
  impact but also demonstrate their commitment to sustainability to their
  customers and stakeholders.
