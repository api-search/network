  Microsoft Azure Document Intelligence, formerly known as Form Recognizer, is a
  powerful tool that allows organizations to extract valuable information from a
  variety of documents. With its advanced machine learning capabilities,
  Document Intelligence can accurately analyze and process forms, invoices,
  receipts, and other types of documents. By automatically recognizing and
  extracting data from these documents, businesses can streamline their
  workflows, improve efficiency, and gain valuable insights into their
  operations. Document Intelligence also offers the ability to customize and
  train models to better fit specific document types and formats, making it a
  versatile solution for a wide range of industries. Overall, Microsoft Azure
  Document Intelligence is a valuable tool for organizations looking to automate
  document processing and extract valuable insights from their data.
