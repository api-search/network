description: >-
  The Twilio SendGrid Alerts API allows you to specify an email address to
  receive notifications regarding your email usage or statistics. You can set up
  alerts to be sent to a specific email address on a recurring basis, whether
  for informational purposes or when specific account actions occur.


  For most alerts, you can choose to have the alert sent to you as needed,
  hourly, daily, weekly, or monthly. The information contained in your alert
  will be for the last period of the alert. For example, if you choose weekly
  for the statistics alert, you will receive the statistics for the last week.
