  inventory management and order processing for the retailer. With Product
  Variant Options, retailers can create detailed product listings with multiple
  options, enabling them to showcase their products in a more visually appealing
  and organized way. This feature ultimately enhances the overall shopping
  experience for customers and streamlines the online selling process for
  businesses.
