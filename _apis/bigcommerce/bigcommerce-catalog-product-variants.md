  BigCommerce Catalog - Product Variants is a feature that allows online
  retailers to easily manage and display different versions or options of a
  product on their website. This tool enables merchants to create and track
  various product variants, such as different sizes, colors, or materials, all
  within a single product listing. By using this feature, businesses can
  streamline their inventory management process, improve the shopping experience
  for customers, and increase sales by offering a wider range of choices.
  Additionally, BigCommerce Catalog - Product Variants helps businesses organize
  their products more efficiently, making it easier for customers to find and
  purchase the specific product variations they are looking for.
