description: >-
  BigCommerce Tax Settings allows merchants to easily manage and configure their
  tax settings within their online store. This feature enables businesses to set
  up and apply the appropriate tax rates based on their location, the location
  of their customers, and the type of products being sold. Merchants can also
  specify whether taxes should be included in the product prices or displayed
  separately at checkout. By utilizing BigCommerce Tax Settings, businesses can
  ensure compliance with tax regulations, accurately calculate taxes on sales,
  and provide a seamless and transparent shopping experience for their
  customers.
