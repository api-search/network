  organize and categorize products within their online store. This tool provides
  a visual representation of the product categories and subcategories, making it
  simpler for customers to navigate through the store and find the items they
  are looking for. With BigCommerce Catalog - Category Trees, users can easily
  create, edit, and manage product categories, as well as assign products to
  specific categories. This feature helps improve the overall shopping
  experience for customers and helps increase sales by making it easier for them
  to find and purchase products.
