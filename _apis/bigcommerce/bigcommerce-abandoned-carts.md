description: >-
  BigCommerce Abandoned Carts is a tool designed to help businesses recover
  potentially lost sales by targeting customers who have added items to their
  online shopping cart but have not completed the purchase. This tool sends
  automated emails to remind customers of their abandoned carts and encourages
  them to complete their purchase through personalized messages, discount
  offers, or incentives. By reengaging customers who have shown interest in
  products but have not followed through with a purchase, BigCommerce Abandoned
  Carts helps businesses recover revenue and increase conversions.
