  seamlessly integrate reward points and benefits from their Capital One credit

  exclusive deals, discounts, and offers from a wide range of online retailers,
  all while earning and redeeming their accumulated reward points. This
  innovative tool not only enhances the shopping experience for consumers but
  also provides merchants with valuable insights into customer behavior and
  preferences. By leveraging the power of the Shop with Rewards API, businesses
  can drive sales, boost customer loyalty, and create a more personalized
  shopping experience for their users.
