description: >-
  Captures a previously authorized transaction and initiates the transfer of funds to the merchant. A capture can be for the full authorized amount or a partial amount when split capture is configured. Once captured, the transaction transitions to "captured" status and Affirm initiates the settlement process. Partial captures leave the remaining authorized amount available for subsequent captures.
